Welcome to Rishani Food Products

“Rishani Foods” an upcoming Brand in the field of Confectionery and bakery segment, with the motto of providing highest quality food products with best of hygiene standards to the customer to ensure the best return of customer’s investment.

“Rishani” a name that itself defines the Quality of the product, “The word RISHANI stands for Rich in Sweet, Healthy, Attractive, Nutritious and Intense.

Rishani Food Products, a company owned by the Poria’s, one of the market leaders in the Biscuit Industry.

Commenced in the year 2022, “Rishani Foods” has entered in the market as a leading Manufacturer of Biscuits, Cake, Rusk Toast, Milk Rusk, Cardamom Rusk, Suji Toast etc. Company started initially from Paschim Medinipur origin, with well-equipped factory unit with high advanced technology machines which will help to produce quality products and develop new products for the bakery industry.
